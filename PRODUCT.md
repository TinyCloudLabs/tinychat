# Product

## Register

product

## Users

TinyCloud Chat is for people who want a practical AI chat client without giving the chat provider permanent control over their conversation history. Users sign in with a passkey, chat across models, and expect their threads to follow them across devices through their own TinyCloud space.

## Product Purpose

TinyCloud Chat demonstrates a full TinyCloud app: OpenKey browser identity, TinyCloud-backed storage, backend session verification, model routing, confidential inference, and delegated access patterns. Success means the app feels like a normal fast chat tool while making ownership, portability, and verifiability concrete.

## Brand Personality

Restrained, technical, trustworthy. The tone should be plainspoken and confident, with privacy claims tied to visible mechanisms rather than broad slogans.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic AI chatbot marketing, decorative protocol diagrams that do not explain a real workflow, crypto jargon as a substitute for product clarity, and loud enterprise dashboards that foreground infrastructure over the chat experience.

## Design Principles

- Lead with the user workflow, then disclose the protocol detail where it matters.
- Treat privacy as an interaction model, not a tagline.
- Keep the chat surface quiet, compact, and repeat-use friendly.
- Make trust inspectable through concrete details: passkeys, user-owned storage, delegations, and attestation.
- Prefer small, verifiable claims over broad platform promises.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use semantic controls, keyboard-reachable dialogs and actions, readable contrast in light and dark themes, and reduced-motion-safe interaction patterns. Sharing and sign-in flows should surface errors in plain language and never rely on color alone.
